Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

Overview

ChartGame ("the App") is developed by XYZsLabs. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

Data We Collect

Anonymous Account

When you first use the App, an anonymous account is created automatically using Firebase Authentication. No email, phone number, or real name is required. You choose a display name (pseudonym) to appear on leaderboards.

Game Performance Data

We collect your game results, including return on investment (ROI), portfolio value, and win/loss records. This data is used to power the leaderboard and daily challenge rankings.

Data Stored on Your Device Only

The following data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Profile photo
  • Full game history
  • Achievements and streaks
  • App preferences and settings

Data Shared with Leaderboard

When you play games, the following is submitted to our leaderboard (hosted on Firebase Firestore):

  • Your display name (pseudonym)
  • Aggregate performance stats (ROI, win rate, net worth, games played)
  • Daily challenge results
  • Follow relationships (who you follow)

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google) — Used for anonymous authentication, leaderboard storage, and app configuration. Firebase Analytics and Ads are disabled.
  • Finnhub — Used to fetch market news articles. No user data is sent to Finnhub.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Email address, phone number, or real name
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, UDID)
  • Contacts, calendar, or health data
  • Browsing history
  • Analytics or advertising data

Data Deletion

You can reset your account at any time within the App. This deletes your Firebase account, profile photo, and follow relationships. You can also delete the App to remove all locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since no personal information is required to use the App, we have no way to identify the age of our users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub Issues page.
