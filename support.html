Frequently Asked Questions

How does ChartGame work?

You're shown a real historical stock or crypto chart. You make buy and sell decisions as the chart reveals bar by bar. At the end, your return is compared against a simple buy-and-hold strategy.

Do I need to create an account?

No. The app creates an anonymous account automatically. No email, phone number, or personal information is required.

Is real money involved?

No. ChartGame is a simulation for practice and education only. No real money is ever at risk.

How do I reset my account?

Go to your profile in the app and use the reset account option. This will delete your leaderboard data and create a fresh anonymous account.

What is the Daily Challenge?

Every day, all players get the same chart to trade. Results are ranked on a daily leaderboard so you can see how your skills compare.
